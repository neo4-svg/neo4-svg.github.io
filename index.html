About Cyber SH

Cyber SH was started in late 2026 with the vision of creating AI that belongs to no corporation, no gatekeeper — only to the community. We focus on privacy, resilience, and hacker-driven innovation.

About the CEO

Neo4, founder and CEO of Cyber SH, is a professional hacker and pro-Linux user. The project was born from a desire to build AI that respects independence and transparency. With years of experience in cybersecurity and open-source development, Neo4 leads Cyber SH with discipline and vision.

Our Team

Cyber SH is built by a diverse team of contributors worldwide. From the earliest days, our team has focused on creating tools that empower developers and researchers. Collaboration and open-source culture drive our progress.

How We Train AI

We take core engines from major companies and retrain them with our own datasets, vision, and security-focused adjustments. This ensures Cyber SH AI is aligned with our mission: fast, private, and community-driven.
