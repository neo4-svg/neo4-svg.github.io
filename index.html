About Cyber SH

AI you can run, read, and trust.

We started Cyber SH because the fastest AI shouldn't require handing your data to someone else's server. Everything we ship is built to run locally, inspected openly, and improved in public.

Mission

We're here for local AI — power and privacy, without compromise.

Vision

We will always push for AI that's both genuinely powerful and genuinely private. Speed and control aren't a trade-off — they're the standard.

Privacy

Your data and your models stay on your machine — not on ours.

Transparent

Source, training notes, and decisions are public — nothing is a black box.

Local

Designed to run on hardware you own, with no mandatory cloud round-trip.

Always supported

Active maintenance and a community that keeps showing up for the project.

What you get

Built to run on your terms.

Every feature traces back to the same idea: capable AI shouldn't require giving up control of your data or your hardware.

01 / OFFLINE

Runs fully offline

No mandatory account, no required internet connection. Once a model is on your machine, it stays usable without phoning home.

02 / OPEN

Open weights & code

Model weights, training notes, and source are public on GitHub — inspect them, fork them, or audit them yourself.

03 / HARDWARE-AWARE

Built for real hardware

Models are trained and tuned with consumer-grade hardware in mind, not just data-center GPUs.

04 / NO TELEMETRY

No telemetry by default

Nothing is collected, logged, or sent anywhere unless you explicitly turn it on.

05 / RED-TEAMED

Reviewed before release

Every model is tested by experienced practitioners for quality and safety before it ships — see how on the training page.

06 / COMMUNITY

Community-governed direction

Roadmap priorities are shaped by contributors and users, not a closed product team.

How we train AI

Trained on real hardware. Reviewed by real experts.

Every model we ship goes through the same three-stage process — no shortcuts, no unreviewed releases.

01

Train

Models are trained on our own GPU farm, on data curated for the specific capability we're building — nothing generic, nothing outsourced.

02

Evaluate

Every model is tested and red-teamed by experienced practitioners before it goes anywhere near a release branch.

03

Ship

Once a model clears review for quality and alignment, it's published openly — built for the best real-world experience, not a benchmark score.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

If something's missing here, ask directly — see the contact section below.

Is Cyber SH really free?+
Yes. Cyber SH is donation-supported — there's no paywall on the models or the source code.
Do I need a powerful GPU to run your models?+
No. Models are tuned with consumer-grade hardware in mind, not just data-center GPUs. Requirements vary by model — check each release's notes on GitHub.
How is this different from cloud-based AI?+
Cloud AI sends your data to someone else's server to get an answer. Cyber SH models run on your own machine, so your data never has to leave it.
Is any of my data collected?+
No telemetry is collected by default. Nothing is logged or sent anywhere unless you explicitly enable it.
How can I contribute?+
Start on GitHub — open issues, submit pull requests, or help test new releases. Reach out directly if you want to get involved beyond code.
